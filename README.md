# Starfish by Peter Watts

A huge international corporation has developed a facility along the Juan de Fuca Ridge at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean to exploit geothermal power. They send a bio-engineered crew -- people who have been altered to withstand the pressure and breathe the seawater -- down to live and work in this weird, fertile undersea darkness. The crew are society's detritus: disgraced, dangerous, damaged people who prove to be the only ones able to adapt to the Rifter lifestyle.

First published in 1999 by Tor Books. This is the first novel in the Rifters trilogy.
